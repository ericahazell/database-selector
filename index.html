The Boolean Basics

Use these three operators to combine your keywords:

  • AND narrows your search by requiring ALL terms to be present
    diabetes AND exercise
    Returns only articles that discuss both diabetes and exercise
  • OR broadens your search by finding ANY of your terms
    adolescent OR teenager OR youth
    Returns articles that mention any of these terms (useful for synonyms!)
  • NOT excludes terms you don't want
    cancer NOT lung
    Returns cancer articles but excludes anything about lung cancer

Quick Tips for Better Results

  • Think in concepts: Break your research question into 2-3 main concepts
  • Use synonyms: Connect similar terms with OR (therapy OR treatment OR intervention)
  • Keep it simple: Start with a basic search, then refine based on what you find
  • Fewer is better: 2-4 well-chosen keywords usually work better than 10
